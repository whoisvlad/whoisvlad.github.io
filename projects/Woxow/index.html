Onze Missie

Woxow started for the love of programming, traveling and networking. woxow aims to provide support for Internet companies and advertising agencies. The Internet is increasingly growing, Annual more and more webshops and websites starts. With the advent of mobile the demand will increase sharply to online services.

With the personal approach as a freelancer, but the certainty of an established company, woxow offers support from the best of both worlds.

Our focus is mainly on Wordpress and Magento support. Both platforms provide a range of possibilities. By focusing us on these parts, we can provide custom work and develop our knowledge getting better.
